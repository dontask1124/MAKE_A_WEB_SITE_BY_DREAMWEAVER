Interior

If there is one thing we in the carwow office can agree, it’s that the Mercedes A45 AMG comes equipped with an excellent pair of front seats. They hold you in place when cornering quickly, are supportive and set low to give a sporty feel. The AMG steering wheel is a pleasure both to hold and look at.

The dashboard looks smart too, with sporty flashes of carbon-fibre trim featuring red inserts. It’s somewhat over the top but at the least the A45’s inners could never be accused of being boring.
Exterior

The Mercedes A45 AMG 2022 is aimed at a young audience, so the exterior design of the Mercedes A 45 AMG is youthful and sporty.

The Mercedes A 45 AMG impresses with the AMG sports front bumper covered in titanium gray below, the black air intake splint and the grille with 2 AMG spokes in titanium gray with the 3-pointed star logo in the middle. The Mercedes A 45 AMG front light cluster uses Bi-xenon type with integrated LED daytime running lights.

Compared to previous versions, spoilers have also been added under the front bumper, roof spoiler, and rear diffuser for better aerodynamics.

The Mercedes A 45 AMG also has a distinctive look with the word 'AMG' on the rear doors, 'TURBO AMG' badging on the sides and door sill panels are also polished with the 'AMG' badge. 18-inch 5-spoke dual-spoke AMG sports wheels in polished titanium gray.
ENGINE

The new Mercedes A 45 AMG 4Matic 2022 engine uses the familiar turbocharged 2.0L cylinder engine of the A-class. This new engine block provides a capacity of up to 380 hp at 6000 rpm and maximum torque of up to 450 Nm at 2250-5000 rpm.

If regular versions like A200 or A250 are only equipped with front-wheel drive, the A45 AMG 2022 is equipped with the famous 4MATIC 4-wheel drive system. With this system, the vehicle's handling and driving feel will be greatly improved, especially at high speeds.

AMG SPEEDSHIFT DCT 7-speed automatic transmission and 4Matic all-wheel drive help accelerate the car from 0 to 100 km/h in just 4.6 seconds before reaching a top speed of 250 km/h. . The feeling of generous driving is something you can easily feel when "holding the reins" of the A45 AMG.
